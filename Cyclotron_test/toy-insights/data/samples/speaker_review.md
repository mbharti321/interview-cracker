# Wireless Bluetooth Speaker Toy

## Overview
Compact and kid-friendly bluetooth speaker with built-in safety features. Features colorful LED lights and durable design suitable for indoor and outdoor use.

## Parent Review
**4.5 out of 5 stars** - "Great little speaker! My son loves the LED light show that syncs with the music. It's surprisingly loud for its size. Sound quality is decent for a toy. Only wish the battery lasted longer, but at this price point it's hard to complain."

## Specifications
- Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity
- Built-in rechargeable battery (6 hours)
- 3W speaker power
- LED color sync with audio
- Waterproof rating: IPX5
- Weight: 250g
- Multiple color options available

## Great For
Kids aged 5+ who enjoy music and want to play their favorite songs wirelessly.
